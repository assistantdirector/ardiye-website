Lost Frequency

Year 2024
Type Video Art
Artist Cemre Yılmaz
[ PROJECT MEDIA ]
Director Cemre Yılmaz
Sound Ardiye Team
Location Old Factory

Lost Frequency is an exploration of the unseen noise in the urban landscape. Recorded over 40 days in abandoned industrial zones, the piece questions the permanence of auditory memory.

The visual language relies on glitch aesthetics and grainy textures to represent the decay of the physical space. As the viewer moves through the corridors of the factory, the sound becomes a guide, yet an unreliable one.
