Narrative

Ardiye is not a gallery. We are a storage unit for the incomplete. We collect the raw, the unpolished, and the forgotten.

Ardiye functions as an archive for projects that were never meant to end. We provide a space where the process is valued over the result.

Our residents do not aim for perfection; they aim for documentation. Here, every scratch, glitch, and noise is part of the inventory.
