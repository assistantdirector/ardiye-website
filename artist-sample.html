Cemre Yılmaz

Visual Artist / Filmmaker

Cemre Yılmaz (b. 1996) works with video, sound, and installation to explore the relationship between urban memory and decay.

Her works often focus on abandoned spaces, using them as a canvas to question the temporality of human existence. She is a co-founder of Ardiye Initiative.
