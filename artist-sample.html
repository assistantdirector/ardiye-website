Cemre Yılmaz

Visual Artist / Filmmaker

Cemre Yılmaz (b. 1996, İzmir) works with video, sound, and installation to explore the relationship between urban memory and decay.

Her works often focus on abandoned spaces, using them as a canvas to question the temporality of human existence. She is a co-founder of Ardiye Initiative.

She has exhibited her works in various independent art spaces across Turkey and Europe.
